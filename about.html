About Me

I am a passionate innovator and media student at Mount Kenya University, Thika Campus. As the founder of BioVotex Technologies, I developed the award-winning Smart Fingerprint Ballot Box, securing victories in the Young Scientist Kenya 2022 and MKU Innovation Challenge 2023.

Beyond technology, I serve as the Public Relations Officer for the Taita Taveta Students Association (TTSA), mentor young students, and actively support climate and community initiatives.
